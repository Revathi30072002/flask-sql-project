Birth and education:


  Chawla, who chose India as his homeland during the Indo-Pakistani partition, disembarked at Karnal in Haryana and boarded a train from Pakistan to New Delhi. Kalpana Chawla was born on 1.7.61 as his beloved daughter. Kalpana Chawla graduated from the Punjab College of Engineering in 1982 after completing her schooling at Tagore Balaniketan. Her wedding took place on December 2 of the same year. She received her master's degree in aerospace engineering from the University of Texas in 1984. She received her doctorate in 1988 in the same course.


Work experiences:


  Kalpana's space dream was ridiculed and tested, but she did not care in the slightest. In 1988, she joined NASA's Ames Research Center. She has served as assistant president and research scientist at Los Aldas Myths Inc. in California. She married Jin Piercaricin, who trained him to fly.


Try flying:


  In 1993 she applied to fly to the NASA Space Center as an astronaut. The result was a failure. However, she thought defeat was the first step to success and further qualified herself in 1994 when she applied and succeeded. She then received special training in Kentucky in 1994. She joined the Johnson Space Center in 1995 and joined the 15th Space Flight Corps. Trained at NASA in 1996. In the same year she received the title of Flight Specialist.


Space travel:


  He made his first voyage from November 19 to December 5, 1997, across the country, language and borders. During this 65 lakh mile journey, she orbited the earth 257 times. The journey time is 376 hours and 34 minutes. The SDS Columbia spacecraft, Kalpana's second and final voyage, exploded on February 1, 2003, 16 minutes before landing on the 16th day after completing 80 missions in space. And Kalpana's space dreams are half-scattered. The world lost a rare brave astronaut.


Other tasks:

Conclusion:
