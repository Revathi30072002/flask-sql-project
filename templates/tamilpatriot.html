Introduction:


  Pavender Bharathidasan was the one who realized the sweetness, youth and taste of Tamil. Tamil is alive and well. There is no one who loves Tamil like him. There is no one who has revolutionized the world of Tamil poetry like him. We will see the unique Tamil heart of that Tamil citizen who lived in Tamil.


Tamil life:


  Pavender saw Tamil as the highest in the world.
   "The milk given by cow
   Cold water given by coconut
   Though they are sweet
   But Tamil is my life"
  - You can know his love for Tamil from what Bharathidasan mentions. Not only that
   "Sweet Tamil language is ours - for us
   It is like a plesure giving elixir."
  - By saying that he describes Tamil as a life-enhancing elixir.
   "Tamil is the milk of our youth - this
   Pleasure tamil is equal to our life."
  - Saying that he admires to keep his life net.


Lullaby for Mother Tamil:


  It is the nature of the world for a mother to praise her child. But Bharathidasan himself praises the Tamil mother. He describes tamil as,    Elixir, Awesome, Beautiful,
   Pleasure, Life, Attractice,
   Fruit, Pot, Honesty
   Unique, Sacrificer, Admirable
   Fire, Honey, Spontaneous,
   Southern, Fullest, Benefit,
   Old, Mass, Overflowing,
   Mother, Earth - By praising that many, Pavender shows that Tamil is indelibly filled in his heart.


Tamil application:


  Bharathidasan murmured that the Tamil component that rules itself should rule the good world    "The pure temple should have everything in Tamil
    Every office work should be in Tamil
    Commerce board point gate should be printed in Tamil
    Oppurtunity and Tamil everywhere"
  -Bavender speaks Tamil throughout Tamil Nadu with this song. "My mouth greets pure Tamil in case of sleep deprivation" - he says that he won't forget Tamil even if he forgets his own identity.


Tamil Security:


   "There is no abolitionist in the world
   The anti-Tamil has never lived"
   -exclaims Bharathidasan.
   "It is not in vain that our breath is to pluck the silverware of Tamil life"
  - Thus he fully identifies the Tamil mania that arose in his heart


The antiquity of Tamil is special:


   "The first man to appear was a Tamil
    The first language is Tamil - hence
    The root of new life is Tamil culture"
  "You have seen our pleasant Tamil language since the day it appeared. Palanthamil is the day on which the intellectual poet appeared on that day." - from theses lines Bharathidasan telling the atiquity of Tamil.


Conclusion:


  We can see his Tamil heart not only in his works Kudumba Vilakku, Irunda Veedu, Revolutionary, Sanjeeva Parvathathin Charal, Pandian Parisu, Pichiranthaiyar, Azhagin Siripu, etc. '' We can know. We will love and think of Tamil like him; Let's live like Tamil.
