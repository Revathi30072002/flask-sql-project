Birth and Education:


  Jawaharlal Nehru was born in Allahabad, on the banks of the Ganges, on Monday, November 14,1889 as the son of Motilal Nehru and Soruba Rani.


  Nehru learned elementary education at home and excelled in learning English novels and Science subjects. He then went on to study at the Harrow Education Center in the UK where he was not allowed to tal about Indian politics. So Nehru did his B.A.Honors degree from Trinity College under the supervision of the University of Cambridge. He then studied law at the Innder Temple University and returned to India in 1912 with the title of barrister. While studying ain London he listened to the speeches of Vipin Chandrapal Gokhale and Lala Lajpat Rai and became thirsty for freedom.
